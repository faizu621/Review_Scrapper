"Has always been a great CRM but not the best for companies without the costly expertise"
What do you like best about Salesforce Sales Cloud?

I appreciate the overall setup of the system, including its layout and the way it integrates with other platforms. The detailed reporting also stands out, as it allows you to delve deeply into the data. Customer support has always ben great and we use this everyday! Review collected by and hosted on G2.com.

What do you dislike about Salesforce Sales Cloud?

Salesforce is deeply complex, and unless you have someone who is at least an admin, you won't be able to access more than the basic metrics. Tracking custom metrics can be extremely challenging if you don't have someone with significant experience in the nuances of Salesforce. Not only is Salesforce itself expensive, but hiring the right people to operate it can be even more costly. Implementation of Salesforce products can be very time consuming and costly and Salesforce itself is not always easy to use for less technical users. Review collected by and hosted on G2.com.

What problems is Salesforce Sales Cloud solving and how is that benefiting you?

This platform enables you to carry out many functions in a single location, which can simplify things for users who are adept at managing it and for leaders who are clear about the information they require. However, this isn't always the reality, as the out-of-the-box reporting is, at best, below expectations. Review collected by and hosted on G2.com.
